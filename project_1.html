Sistem Pendukung Keputusan Kelayakan Pemberian Kredit Pada LPD Desa Bekul

Decision Support System which aims to facilitate the LPD, especially in the credit section, in making decisions regarding customers who wish to apply for credit. This system uses the SAW (Simple Additive Weighting) method, which determines the feasibility of giving credit decisions based on predetermined criteria and values. There are 2 users, namely the staff of the credit department and the head of the LPD, system testing uses black box testing.
