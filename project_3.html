Aplikasi E-Booking Jasa Rias Pengantin Bali Pada Dewi Sri Salon & Spa Menggunakan Framework Codeigniter

The e-booking application for makeup services at Dewi Sri Salon & Spa aims to make it easier for salons to provide fast and precise service responses, to help increase the productivity of Balinese bridal makeup services based on the number of transactions that occur, as a tool to make it easier for salon customers to choose and determine the desired make-up services wherever and whenever they want. Make it easy for salons to make reports on booking make-up services because they are already stored in the database. There are 3 users who have different access rights, namely Admin, User, and member
