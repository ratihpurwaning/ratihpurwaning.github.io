Aplikasi Pengolahan Data Kerja Praktek pada Dinas Kominfo dan Statistik Kota Denpasar

This practical work data processing application aims as a means to assist and facilitate officers in processing student/student practical work data, Facilitating students in obtaining announcements and information regarding practical work at the Office of Communications and Information and Statistics of the City of Denpasar, Assisting supervisors of practical work in agencies in checking student data/student practical work and providing an assessment of students/student practical work. There are 3 (users), which have different functions, including Admin, coaches, and students. Testing this system using black box testing.
